CareNow is a tech-enabled home and mobile healthcare platform designed to coordinate real-world care services in one ecosystem. Combines personal support, medical access, medication adherence, wellness oversight, meal services, and family/provider coordination.

**Why:** Carl Ward, Brandon's brother-in-law, passed from lung cancer and needed this kind of support. The gap between hospital discharge and daily living is where CareNow operates.

**How to apply:** CareNow is part of the Michael AI product portfolio. When discussing Michael AI products or Hughes Technologies, include CareNow. It targets Huntsville, AL as launch market with potential Huntsville Hospital integration.

**Core services:** CNA/home care, mobile doctor visits, daily medication passes, wellness checks, meal prep, meal kit distribution, on-demand dietary-specific meal delivery.

**Target users:** Patients (elderly, chronic illness, post-surgery, mobility-limited), families, caregivers/CNAs, medical providers, hospital systems.

**Positioning:** Investor-grade healthcare infrastructure opportunity, $100M+ projected value narrative, Apple-style presentation discipline.

**Pitch:** Hospitals need a system that helps patients succeed AFTER treatment. CareNow extends the chain of care into the home.
